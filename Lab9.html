Analysis

This scatterplot plots the murder rates in 2014 of different cities, and I annotated the cities with the highest murder rates. These data reflects the usual expectation about crime rates in big cities like New York, Chicago, Los Angeles, etc. By plotting the murder rates against x-values of different states, we can also notice that cities in the same state can have a high difference in murder rates. For instance, in Texas there are cities with less than 50 murder rates and there’s Houston with almost 250 murders.
